About the Band

Stage Fright is a high-energy rock band that thrives on pushing boundaries and breaking the silence with their electrifying performances. Each member brings a unique, fearless style, from intense guitar riffs to powerful rhythms that leave a lasting impact. With their unapologetic sound and unrelenting passion, StageFright is here to dominate the stage and captivate every audience.

Jack Harper - Lead Vocalist

Jack brings electrifying energy to the stage with his masterful guitar solos. He is Known for his dynamic performances and creative riffs, he is the heart of Stage Fright's sound.

Maya Kapoor - Guitarist

Maya’s rhythm guitar skills add depth and harmony to the band's music. Her ability to infuse modern sounds with soulful melodies sets the band apart.

Sofia Ramirez - Drummer

Sofia keeps the band’s beats alive with her vibrant drumming. Her energetic rhythms and precise timing fuel every performance, captivating the crowd.
