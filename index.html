About the Band

Stage Fright is an up-and-coming rock band known for their electrifying performances and soul-stirring music...

Jack Harper - Lead Vocalist

Jack brings electrifying energy to the stage with his masterful guitar solos. Known for his dynamic performances and creative riffs, he is the heart of Stage Fright's sound.

Maya Kapoor - Guitarist

Maya’s rhythm guitar skills add depth and harmony to the band's music. Her ability to infuse modern sounds with soulful melodies sets the band apart.

Sofia Ramirez - Drummer

Sofia keeps the band’s beats alive with her vibrant drumming. Her energetic rhythms and precise timing fuel every performance, captivating the crowd.
